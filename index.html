Rev M.P. Malatjie Primary School

Rev M.P. Malatjie Primary School was established in 1994 by the former Lebowa Government as one of its Schools of Excellence. It was named after the late Rev M.P. Malatjie who was one of the freedom fighters during the apartheid era.
